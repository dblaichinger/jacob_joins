Hello!

We’d like to invite you to participate in Jacob Joins! This global cooking project, created by eight students from Salzburg
in Austria, should give an authentic impression of the different eating habits

and cultures from all over the world. Support our project and expand your culinary horizon by following these three steps! We’re not looking for the perfect recipe, but rather want you to be creative.

How to participate

1.

Prepare a meal that contains the following ingredients: Potato, Onion & Egg.

2.

Add your favorite, customary spices and as many other ingredients as you want.

3.

Take pictures of the process and your final meal.

Finally upload your recipe and pictures to www.jacobjoins.com

Please upload your recipe until May 31st!

If the project keeps running until June, we are going to start a donation campaign. For each uploaded recipe our partner companies will transfer a donation to a selected organisation.

I am so excited to read and try your recipe!
